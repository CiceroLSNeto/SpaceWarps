Gravitational Lens Playground

Below are three images from the CFHTLS survey, that do not contain any gravitational lenses. But what would the images look like if there really was a distant faint blue galaxy right behind one of the massive yellow galaxies shown? Well, you can find out by moving your mouse over each image. Have fun, spotters!

In the two images above, we've zoomed in on two types of lens, a massive galaxy (above left) and a cluster of galaxies (above right). To see these lenses in context, we've simulated the lensing effects in one of the CFHTLS survey images, on the right. Notice the size of the arcs and rings!

The LensToy software used in these simulations can be found at https://github.com/slowe/LensToy
