Welcome to SpaceWarps

We are a Zooniverse project, to be launched in Spring 2013, that will crowd-source the discovery of gravitational lenses. The science team are developing a number of prototype webpages to help our volunteers learn about gravitational lenses - what they look like, and how they work - as quickly as possible.

The Spotter's Guide

We want to show people what lenses look like - and what they don't! The Spotter's Guide will be an interactive gallery of both known lenses and some common sources of confusion, that are relevant to the current survey's data.

Under construction...

Gravitational Lens Playground

We have built a little lens simulator, called "LensToy," to show people very quickly what gravitational lenses do - you place a source "behind" a massive galaxy in the image shown to you, and LensToy shows you what the corresponding lensed features would look like.

Under construction...

Gravitational Lens School

We'd like people to understand a bit about how spacewarps work - so on the School page, we try and explain the physics of gravitational lensing.'
